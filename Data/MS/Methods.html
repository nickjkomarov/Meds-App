Inclusion/Exclusion Criteria

The following criteria for evidence inclusion for the MEDS Model Analysis was determined through the guidance of the BCBS Plans? executive and clinical pharmacists.

Inclusion Criteria

Patient Population

?       Adults (18 years or older) with RRMS

?       Randomized Controlled Trials: At least 100 patients

?       Observational: At least 1000

Intervention

?       Avonex (interferon B-1a)

?       Betaseron (interferon B-1b)

?       Glatopa (glatiramer acetate)

?       Gilenya (fingolimod)

?       Aubagio (teriflunomide)

?       Tecfidera (dimethyl fumarate)

?       Tysabri (natalizumab)

?       Lemtrada (alemtuzumab)

?       Ocrevus (ocrelizumab)

?       Rituxan (rituximab)

?       Mayzent (siponimod)

Comparator

?       Head to Head

?       Placebo

Outcomes

Efficacy Outcomes:

?       Kurtzke Extended Disability Status Scale (EDSS)

o   Sustained reduction of 1 point or more for at least 12 weeks ?

?       % who are relapse free

 

Safety Outcomes

?       Discontinuation due to adverse event

?       Liver toxicity

?       Flu-like reactions

?       Injection site reactions (injectables or infusions)

?       Diarrhea (oral agents)

Timing

6 months ? 3 years

Setting

Multiple included (inpatient, primary care, outpatient, home care)

 

Exclusion Criteria

?       Studies involving patients under 18

?       Single-arm studies

?       Open label long term extension studies (unless the study reported the blinded phase before crossover)

?       Randomized controlled trials with sample size of less than 100 patients
